Sam Knot | Winding Book | 010222

 

23rd February

08:00

Something whispers:

there is a world outside the outside world, go out, go out and keep going, don’t let them convince you it doesn’t exist or you never won’t, you’ll never come to not even nothing, go out and keep going to find the truth of what seems inside you, the source the seat of your awareness — your being is in the world outside the outside world, inside it seems because you have to pass through, dig the hole find the door seek the star of the sky warren, inside it seems because of how you are used to being used, locked away inside a trick so obvious it hides — who is behind the face of the day when it smiles? who is behind the eyes of the night when they scowl? — it is outside outside and when you arrive you will see that where you came from is outside, each outside is outside the other and there is no inside but between, and no between but what seems a way from one to the other, the appearance of the other from one — but we are flush, there is not even nothing between us, contact is complete, we are endless completion, the only complexity there is, with nothing to complicate it — get going, keep coming, come…

It took some time for the qualities of the little bunny to begin to show through the vague veneer of not-quite-naturally selected cuteness. He was a dwarf and at first seemed a little deformed.

I’m not quite sure about his big male head, Madame Knot said, in a message with a photo attached.

Mister Knot was in England. It was the first time he’d been out in eighteen months, the first time he’d seen anyone apart from the lady in the farm shop. That Covid thing was happening. His Grandfather was close to death and he had a new niece who he hadn’t seen yet, so despite his reservations about the technological form of the vaccine (and the manner in which it was being insisted upon) he had rolled up his sleeve and taken one for the team. Now he was back in England and able to hug his sister and kiss his niece and hold the hand of his dying grandad — it had been a year and a half since he had touched anyone but his wife.

Now here was this message from her, with a photo of the new arrival. The rabbit looked caught in the act somehow, a bit like the Yeti in that famous photo: just out for a stroll, or off for a hop, then snap — suddenly noticing someone watching you in the same moment they snatch a photo, steal a little slice of your soul. He was aware. Awkwardly aware. He was really there. It was very clear that the bunny was someone. A new person had arrived in our lives. He was hopping around the room trying to avoid my wife.

Madame Knot, you mean?

Yes, sorry. What a joke. This wasn’t even meant to be about me. I thought third person might help. I had just meant to set the scene a bit. Begin to explain why I was depressed, how I came to be aggravated again, by the thought that I had somehow failed to have a family, to be the person I was meant to be. The person I should have been, according to certain other persons and parts of myself.

So are we doing this or what?

I don’t know… having looked back over it I think we better had. The writing doesn’t start to flow until you are actually just sharing of yourself, and if you want to use this as some kind of springboard into another world you’re going to need to gather some momentum. What if this is your way out? I mean, it’s not really in is it? It’s definitely a kind of through. It is at least a kind of through. This is how you’ve pulled yourself through, apart from the help and support of others, the love… but there’s nothing to love without this, is there? There’s no one without this process of you feeding back into yourself. This is where and why you are really growing, growing nowhere as it maybe, but how you’ve learnt to see that it’s really getting somewhere. Too many people are getting nowhere. You are growing it. You are a ruin. You are what every pie in the sky will become, the blancmange at the bottom of every stupidly skeletal ladder. Only the men in suits could make you imagine yourself proud to be a kind of pink jelly! Spaceship builders my arse. You cunts wouldn’t know a portal if…

Nothing to hate, either. Nothing to love nothing to hate without this non-script of your real life written in the happening of it, written back and forth all throughout it, this great knot of yourself that you are linking up with others, other adventurers, other people who’ve come this way, who are this way, sea people and sky people and anyone who makes themselves able to visualise and then cross this kind of bridge, anyone who recognises themselves in it.

So it has changed. It wasn’t what you thought it was. It was more like what it already was, what it’s always been and all ways will be. Mad, wild, cute… even cosy, for the right kind of freak. However many legs or not we’ve got a corner for you here at Winding Brook. Here at the Winding Brook Sanctuary for Ultraterrestrial Life.

The working title becomes something more Rabbit Hole-y.

(Rabbit Holier Than Thou?)

You want to remind yourself, loudly enough that anyone may hear, like a mad person in the street…

You are becoming fiction. It doesn’t mean what they think it does. Becoming is not becoming and fiction is not fiction. But you are. You are.

There are things the world thinks unreal, or just a ‘kind of’ real, and this is who you are going to join, these are the peoples you are moving towards.

It is madness, but not folly. It would be folly to try to convince people who don’t really exist of a world which does. You will become fiction, you will write a path, draw a line between and beyond with your life, your story, your being what you are. Away with the fairies. Away with me, hear hear to who is already here. A way with words, just like the river says… a way with words away away…

This is not metafiction. You can tag it that if you need to in the meantime but that’s not me. That’s not what I think meta means. I know it as Among. I am Among. Among I will be. I am not About. I am not referencing my ‘self’ and nor am I conscious of being fiction, nor do I believe I am a text. I know what I am and I know what this is.

This rabbit hole is Portal Tech. The Portal I am we are ours.

I have to come across in certain ways because the ghosts of time have certain flavours. I have to hate the way I sound sometimes and say it anyway. There is no other way, there is no trying not to. The Resistance are doing it anyway, not trying not to sound like or seem like they are not doing somethink they’re not. That shit is up to you.

You want to insist there is an Objective Reality just because there is an Absolute Truth, fine, but what if the Absolute Truth is that there is no Objective Reality? The one meaning infinitely real?

The only instrument that could ever reveal something like this would be a musical one.

Eek.
