Dreamt of swimming in my Grandparent’s house. Or Glastonbury Goddess water (drinking a little Chalice Well water every day, from the bottle we filled). Or absence of local electromagnetic fields in the form of wi-fi courtesy Lightning Strike. Ptchao!
