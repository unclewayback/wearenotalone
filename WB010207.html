We begin from finity, which is the forgetting or eclipse of its own source. We develop a concept of infinity ‘in light’ of this finity, and that is what we take infinity to be. So, perhaps I should say finity is the forgetting and infinity is the eclipse. There is no bridge from this, no path going back — we cannot salmon swim back upstream to find the real or true infinity — this is the Abyss — here it is. It never left, except to provisionally become left, if that can be at all helpful, if not fine, it’s alright, forget about it.

The Actual Infinity, which we could call The Virtual, is what we mean when we say Imagination, so that is what we will call it, because it is closer to our hearts, and more profoundly a part of our own line of work, and it doesn’t intend a set of categories like Virtual Actual Real can seem to. It is like ‘the curve’ of pure exponentiation, as doubling, at root. There is one twin. Then you look at the Prime numbers, and you see how very different the powering of one must be. How very different is unity. It couldn’t even grow, really. Instead it just looks like a patternless thing, something very definite, as definite as the definition of what a prime number is, and yet as chaotic in practice, as essentially non-algorithmic and yet a product of simplicity. You zoom in on the primal ‘curve’ and you see this, you zoom out far enough and its looks just like, exactly the same as, doubling, exponentiation. Here is the other twin.

The problem is that this is not meant to illustrate the two twins, but rather them on one side and what they are not on the other.

It is as if I said: the primes are the infinite, and the ‘squares’ (even three is a cube in this mode), are the finite. But what I wanted to say was: the primal is the definitely infinite, who are in fact the twins, and the exponential is the finite and the infinite as we define them — as they appear to mutually define or delimit each other — which is in fact just a way of knowing who the twins are not, so it is like a kind of twinning the twins are twinned with, but really it has nothing to do with what the twins are, what the twins do, which is the twine-ing of the True, which cannot be not, and so has to be negatively, which should seem impossible, except it is precisely what we try to understand as the possible, the meaning of which is a key to shattering illusion, and seeing everything primally.
