07:38 Winding Brook

Tasting nothing, a glimpse of the spider’s old skin…

Don’t feel like writing, so just processing some old — or not so old in this case — recordings. This is funnest to do when it’s something surprising and interesting I’ve forgotten about, and least fun when it is some long low energy ramble I somehow compel myself to listen to again, just in case. It is only figuring out when to bother that matters — that I don’t get that is sometimes enough to make me want to junk the whole thing off... otherwise, other times, it seems like it could have some value.

But, and this importantly: used sparingly. What I need to achieve is like… a drop of this, then a good wadge of more or less personable straightforward communification — chatting in the most sense-making and welcoming way I can manage — combined with good prose, potent poetry, moments of quiet natural silence, and appropriately paced music.

Because this is the way in, somehow. Or a video, is. This, metaphoreal, is deeper, and the log here the fool plunge… and all this in service of building something else, getting somewhere (grow ling now here twit twoo get her).

& defo learn to worry less about not speaking, leaving space, as well as actively feel (d?) recording — need more textures and stuff: thought was that people will have to be listening, if they are listening, and so will listen to the quiet just as well, or even more so… also a drop of chanting and song is well and good, you is freak, remember — the only appearsons n undorterz who is gonna get you is we.

(recall being so confuzzled by all the relative rights and wrongs of being among the many different loves and strifes we call family, and the That by which one finds ones centre or self in said place — basically the way we weigh emotionally, all those not not logixxx of pheeling that leave us reeling… how it can sometimes seem that any more rational way of decision-making is on the run from this, quite rightly, but also — also this is more the level on which the world seems to (not) work, than the rational. The political seems to be but a hijacked awryness of it, the scientific can’t come near it, and the creative somehow comes out of it (still reeling) — only something like religion (but not religion) might somehow engage it directly, prophet-ably?)

Cold! Strange to catch sight of the new moon just above where the lightning struck hawthorn used to live.

Thinking of poetic reduction. I myself give reductionism short shrift, who doesn’t? But in this age of apparent information… I mean, there is a way in which What elfs is wisdom? I guess this is something more alchemical, like distillation. I was going to say filtration, but we want to lean towards the intensifying aspects, more than the idea of removing anything — the idea being that nothing really important is removed, or lost, anyway.

So this is neither subtractive synthesis, nor additive analysis, I blah.

Clearly it is alchemy, metaphoreally, so why resist? It is life lived and lived. Maybe it is portal tech?

It is not information theoretic. We are not reducing or compressing. We are gaining, by shrugging off — the fundamental moves are quantitive paradoxes, that make perfect qualitive sense.

Apparently Blake’s Vala is very compressed, and this is one of the reasons it is so difficult yet so rewarding. We don’t want to compress, we want to make choices. We want to be aware of… gateways, sure — of paths. To say one can picture the gate, is to imagine the path there for the taking.

But if I carry on like this soon enough I’ll be talking about music and gardening.

That’s the idea.

Also, here in Eurotown, I notice much swaying around Englishness, Britishness, Albionicity… it’s all good, I understand — but it’s not it, not right, not me. I don’t want to make the mistake of that kind of identity, of some sort of misguided nationalism or tribalism — I am also not a globalist, necessarily. Anyway… for me what I am is something I am, and one can certainly see national-seeming characteristics — English eccentricity or a bit of British, what? deflationism or something… But it is so far from the right way up, to focus on or identify with that. And very dangerous, or just a bit stupid or something, to imagine there is something that needs saving there, by our celebrating it, or whatever. It’s a bit like anarchy, for me: whatever is of a people, itself a blurry and strange and interesting story, in me: it is nothing I can identify with, and nothing I can lose. It is just a way of things, and any attempt to — or most attempts to — bring it out or do something with it, unless ironic or meta in the sense of having some kind of awareness of all these issues, which my Albionic Man was meant to have… except also it was angry about constructionism, wasn’t it? It was saying what I was just saying, about the innateness or just-is-ness of pluralic peopledom, and yet it was using this to argue against social construction, when in fact it is not against such: substrates of things are only against stuff as a foundation is against the building that sits on it… we don’t really want to disidentify then, either, and the not not was at least partly about this. It was our focus in the vast field of the neither nor…

Anyway, you don’t show you are English, or celebrate your Englishness, by flying a flag, or by having a hand-waving statue of the Queen on your kitchen windowsilly — if you are something, you cannot not be it, and this is so important, even as it is ‘given’ (just how it is), that its simplicity needs to be defended: one needs to stay aware / not lose sight of it. Because otherwise ‘people’ will try to make you into something that you already are, and in such processes we can only become what we are not: fakes.

(Those things that we are not that we are, interdependently arising as we, or much of us, or the us of us, be — this, these, is our not not: the metaphoreal. (Stuff like: I am not a squirrel. In f*ct you is not not a squirrel. But: I am proud to be English? You do not even not exist! Something is eating your soul! (Just a General Eye Deer to Beer Ware wiv!)))

The other thing I was thinking is that I would like to get away from this half-here writing, this kind of mad sketching while I’m doing or should be doing something else — but then I was like: but it is fine, as part of your process, it is just not necessarily something you should share. My main criteria should be a kind of care for the reader, in publishing. Perhaps the only way to do this will be to draw a hard line? This, for me, my diary, where I do not have to think that… and the published the published, where all thoughts of my own entertainment or needs are summ-eerily dasmashed?

I think that could be wiser. Then the release schedule we came up with yesterday would be the what we publish, of this process. My diary stays diary unless I say otherwise.

Because I sometimes even wonder if I am being kind to myself, going on as loosely as this. I allow that I am, thinking I get to a stage where this becomes something I move on from. I want to get to a place where even writing my diary, or log, is an act given fully to itself — a prayer or meditation, basically. For this, I smile, not to have to not be silly — it sounds so lofty and serious — it is not about being grave or not — it is just about the degree of presence, of attention. Of care.

This is the ‘dissimilar indifference’ my shnake hisses and lishps aboot, shirley. I give full care and attention to this particuliar mode of sharing which is never not about connection yet must happen largely in, what will probabbley remain mostly unknown as, thee (yes, why knot?) Alone. The sharing is more than immediate, and yet occurring with you who cannot be simply or only here and now. It is according to this, that we feel the core of what we are up to is writing, a kind of writing. The mode of care is very much care, but it is a caring in the mode of paying attention — there is something inherently neutral about it. On another level it will be caring, doing my best to be objective (or simply firm) with myself, so that those of you who do pass this way, do so neither in vanity nor in vain.

For the time being I suppose that what I release will bear more relation to these logs than not, for it is the writing itself I must seek to transform. The aim is an act, and this comes before, or transcends, any idea of utility. Same way we sometimes walk the line, or begin to realize we are walking a line, between cause and effect… or above and beyond them.

Being here and now means more than just taking a few breaths and calming down. Peace means so much more than this. Peace means too much, just rightly.
