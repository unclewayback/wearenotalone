I need to build a kind of sustain pedal for silliness, and just keep it pressed down.

It’s not an easy balance, being this unbalanced.

Serious business, being a fool.

To be earnest and silly, I mean, more particularly. That’s quite a challenging combination, I think. On some level it has to be a fight against something then, but in a way it is a fight against what others might think they are fighting. A serious person is fighting something, the only way to really fight something is seriously, surely? Otherwise it’s marshmallow mallet time? A person in crisis is serious. An existential crisis, for instance, is a serious confrontation with what it means, or is, to be alive andor human or whatever, andor modern etc. Sure, absurdity is part of that. But absurdity isn’t really silly.

I don’t know, really, what I’m on about yet. Only that I seem to knock the wind out my sails often enough, and yet I keep coming back to this place or person. I begin to wonder: if my work somehow depends on creating a certain mood, if it only really works in this small band of possible feelings, or a certain niche of being… then I just need to realise that and do my best to ensure I am creating it. I need to really acknowledge it. Hence silly sustain pedals.

I mean, it is not necessarily an overly restricted emotional range or anything, maybe even too loose, over the top, a bit too much… what do you call it? Ah yes: it is faintly hysterical sometimes, isn’t it? Is it? I think it is, but when I come back into my own fold, it is so, because of some stone wall of unfeeling which is not the holy wall of perfect rage whose face is unmoveable truth (the wall with nothing behind it, the wall that is the void itself). It rails instead, when it does, against masks, surely it does. And of course there are issues with this. Not all masks are bad. A silly sustain pedal would be a kind of mask, only… if a mask could be evaporated and become an atmosphere… if a fart could be a mask. Something inappropriate but funny, in a childish way, perhaps, that you forced out accidentally on purpose.

Hang on a minute, it’s probably just time for a poo.

Anyway, I probably should make a proper study of nonsense, silliness, the absurd. I know I said they are different, absurdity and silliness, but they are certainly part of the same — hmm, sam, I wrote — complex.

I need to make synths sound like birds. (The dawn chorus is on at the minute, you see.) I think perhaps I could move some way towards it using a bit of random and arpeggiation.

I don’t really like beats, even though I do. But I would like to be beatless for the most part — fluid like nature seems, or feels most of the time to me. My heart though, and chant… like stamped feet. Trance. I do want beats, just…

it all has to be right. I went at it very quickly yesterday, necessarily, and one day I will get to that kind of efficiency, but I still have plenty of setting up work to do. A little wonkiness on the pitch helps. It doesn’t have to not be a preset, or presets, just the right ones is all. It’s only sometimes it doesn’t matter… but then all the time is sometimes, which is why it always matters.

He’s not not making sense. Just has a funny way of going about it. Still, we’re probably better off for a bit of that in the longrunning meantime int we?

Have a look at yesterdodd. Try to build a bird machine. Two things you could do today, quite apart from whatever — as we sigh — else needs to be done.

This Team Has An I In (but just to keep an eye out)

My bed is a sledge pulled by husky bunny rabbits
 towards a white-pink sun who never dips below the horizon
 yet never rises higher than the top of me head

The world is covered in snow, or ash from a volcano
 but how could you say no? Her face is what makes the stars
 become actual living metaphors in the dust to dust of everyday

And what else could this light say but that I belong to you
 in the same way that all you really want to give me is myself?
 The truth rages immovably beneath the dream skis of bed sledge,

beneath the ash or snow or moon dust of an undying love
 that has to always mean more than we can ever think it does
 so much that sometimes it will hurt to feel what we most deeply need to mean

And how it has to already be, like the ends of our lives
 plaited together like hair whose end is always there
 but how in living our deaths like this we become anything

but zombies, more like goat’s beards than zombies, more like
 bundles of spider silk wagging like the tail of an impossibly happy dog
 or some well-combed mycelium hungry for an interspecies dialog

Don’t go getting lost, fix your one and only thing to any old beam
 of that dizzy wee star, whose cheeks forever blush with not quite twilight
 and whose well-rounded bounce lights the unforgettable cavern

with the unselfish possibilities of the more than necessary flash
 that photographs the entire moment of the infinite realization
 who would need eternity to develop themselves

were it not for the truth. Were it not for my team and our being
 yours. You fur-wrapped fools with your own same suns to smile upon
 in the fast-dreamt bunny-dash of some catastrophic tiredness

layed out over everything like ash from a volcano or snow or yes!
 A dress. A wedding dress, or a night gown, something you put on
 and take off, like rest or a hat or the face of God

something you imagine
 you could do without
 but you could not
