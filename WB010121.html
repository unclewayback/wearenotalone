Looking in the mirror, brushing my teeth, realizing being among people, here ina babylon, turns me into a preacher. Now that I have my place, my base, my space, outside it all. Now that I know myself. I run up against Babylon and rail. I rub myself up against the Great Whore and whisper in her ear: I know you better than that. You know me better. Let’s not kid ourselves we’re really anything other than holy innocent. As we lie down in the mud, and phuk.

What was I preaching about?

Salvation.

What else?

It is a bit ridiculous, obviously — “Gong is a self-initiation into…” some kind of enlightened spirit blahblah. And absolutely true. It is a proper trip he opens with. It is my trip too. Yours. It will always come down to, and build up to, mind. It actually doesn’t matter if the world goes to shit. If the whole pan flashes. I mean, it does, so much… it is just, somehow, strangely, no good motivation. Not a proper reason. Not solid ground. A wheel-less caravan.

It is the inside that counts. The inside that says there is no in and no out. Only the inside can say this, really. The outside can disappear, get fucked, and we’re all good — every outsider knows this — but the inside goes and it is worse than all gone: it is still here, only not.

This basic orientation, that is as deep as it gets — elementary and beyond elemental — high and low and all this that nowt n spirit n sprites n shut up n shite…

This is Salvation. This is what it always meant to mean. This. A sign post, a general direction. The Idea. All the manys that it means is up to we. It is just the notion, the justesse gist, we have to see.

It was clearer in the mirror, in eye contact, eyebro xpressionz, and the silence of thought… but there is a taste of it here. It is one of those things that we just have to keep saying again and again, because it is more like calling for something, that is so deeply here it is almost always a little lost. More like an emphatic, You know what I mean? Than whatever it is you think that is supposed to be about.

You know what I mean?

Like, the pure gesture, or something. Gesture and glance? But a glance that glances off things, and is not not you looking into yourself, and seeing what cannot be hidden, nor shown. What is.

I suppose there is a sense in which it becomes more apocalyptically salvational, more radically transcendent seeming, the more that other ‘things’, or any ‘things’ are offered as The Answers, as What You Need, or think you want… when we don’t know it is really this ‘nothing’, this thing we are that we cannot have, nor not have… the more the unseen is not seen, the more the unacknowledgeable is not acknowledged, or honoured, perhaps… the more the unshareable is… slighted…
