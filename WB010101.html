Sam Knot | Winding Book | 010101

 

January 31st, 2023

07:19, WB

Not not unlike nothing

Of all her many transformations
 I think I like her best mid-stream
 Here between not just anything
 and a certain kind of everything

Just so cute with her throat of newt
 Ears of goldfish fin and flipper of dolphin
 Nose an unspecific ever-active dangle
 Eyes flickering like a forest glade

And never not that special smile
 Here between the all and all
 Never not that great hall of welcoming
 Beckoning like the sun on cloud or hill

Something always between my view
 and the horizon, every wave on the ocean even
 The house of a moment like this
 A person like you
