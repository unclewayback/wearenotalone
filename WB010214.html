07:49

Happy Valentine’s Day, especially if you are alone.

I am thinking of calling a council of ideas just like you.

The hope for imaginary council?

I don’t know. I had thought not to write today, give myself and everybody else a break. Pop

Regaining My Decomposure

on the ol’ Jukebox and disappear up to my room

climb out the window and just become an up and early worker bee

go buzzing about visiting the primroses, crocuses

not much anyone else about

the violets will be here soon, the daffodils.

Too much gets done without an idea.

Can’t really be a doing without some being in it though can it?

An unbeautiful train station.

Yes, it exists, but it isn’t, really. It is not.

You think because there might have been plans, because that bit fat into thirt, there was an idea behind it? Because people could’ve said what the purpose of it was?

I don’t think so, I honestly don’t.

Only some whys might lead to all wayzzz? But wheel get there in the end, hey?

Then I’m really not thinking of or talking about or aiming for concepts. Or what I’m currently intuitively conceiving of concepts as. I expect this is all quite coherent, more so than it might seem. Also clearly there is a certain amount of correspondance going on. No, no consensus.

But then it really is very serious.

I was once able to conceive, as if an insight, that my mania — excitement, idealism, enthusiasm, but all a little too much, all flying around at thoughtspeed and refusing to stop at the lights — wasn’t different from my depression. Just a different face of it. An attempt to give myself or everybody else a break from it. Or a flight, an escape. Like fantasy.

Like some people think of or use fantasy.

I have no idea how, right now. I figure that if you are in some somewhat perturbed or sensitive state, whatever you read is going to play into that. But it’s not true, no, not the case.

Some books have gods, or ideas, in them. Or they are built like shrines or… even cages — viewing windows or barred portals. Places things — ideas, like us — can meet. Maybe we can only stand either side of a kind of screen, at least to begin with (while we imagine we’re alive perhaps). I don’t know.

(Robert Heinlein can fuck off, by the way, Assignment in Eternity my arse. It’s just a random paperback I got from someone who was chucking a bunch of books away, but there’s no ideas in that. Nothing to animate my death. Just wank.)

Anyway, now I wonder if the insight hasn’t changed to… my silliness, in the modern sense of being a little stupid and finding things generally humorous… isn’t it just a flight from, or abreaction to, how serious this is? How serious I am? How deeply I — we — must mean it?

No, it’s not, I can answer that surely and cleanly and quick. It’s not. But!

I know what you’re saying.

You are profoundly silly, at least you can be or appear it sometimes because it is in fact what or who you are, because the very thing you are serious about has a kind of lightness to it. But it is not exactly, or even exactly not, a This World kind of light.

And you do say stupid or flippant things sometimes. And you do say things you would be better off not saying. And… sometimes reach above your station when you are not careful to feel for the very thing you mean.

You do rush and leap about. You need to just feel it more, let it circulate. It is not everything. It is only something in a very special sense.

Is it you, or

A you, saying that?

This?

This is imaginary council.

Even though I cannot really feel it, or don’t feel it in the way I somehow think that I should… we are in touch.

That’s what the energy is. That’s why or how I can just relax more, chill out a bit. That’s what I’m trying to explore, to teach myself about — to learn from you — and thus that’s really what I’m sharing here, whatever else it seems like.

I guess when it’s wrong, when I get it wrong, I’m not even… not not even… just not sharing. Not the right kind of odd.

I don’t think I’m Zero the Hero am I? I’m not sure that’s you either.

It doesn’t really make sense, it really doesn’t, to do me and you in that way. Me who and you who.

You who?

That was my Granny’s greeting, what she would call out whenever she arrived, came in the open door letting people know she was there.

Yoo hoo!

It’s not a question is it really? Listening isn’t really a question. It’s that glistening feeling. This one, when you notice a breath that was emphasised somehow, an in breath mostly. A breath that was something more than necessary, not necessarily graceful, but gracious, somehow…

Like I imagine a little girl couldn’t be graceful, but how cute it is when she is gracious — and this not being grateful — like that but in the absence of anyone to thank, and very clearly having nothing to be sorry for.

There are tears I may never cry in this book.

They don’t really have a why, like you might think, like I might think, they could have. They could have but they don’t. They are what they are. That is a pure idea, it is true.

I do have pure ideas, but I perceive them… as they are. They are a kind of emotion.

You can’t reason about this, you can’t rationalize it. You can try to talk about it, to let it say stuff with you, but that’s poetry. Poetry would never say you were cold, as if incapable of warming yourself up. It would just kind of slide past — no, you would. You would walk past it. Your eyes would just roll, not even dance, over it.

I wish I was a music teacher.

I wish I had a music teacher.

It’s too late now. Honestly it is. For me it is meant to be like this. I need to play with and learn from the ideas, and hopefully I can find a way to… hopefully you will find a way to this, if it can be at all helpful.

There is no excellence, without learning to be okay about things. Thank you for your council.

I will practise leaving it there for a change.
