07:04

At the edge of the interdependent web
 wherein word means because bird means
 that we are welcome to shut the front door

What Is shuffles into the unspidery nonlight
 to buzz with a chaos of flies
 that should conjure eternal life as much as

An edge or some sort of bridge. But seriously
 Bertie, how could the web, or net, not have an edge?
 Wouldn’t it have to reach some kind of limit

in order to interrelate, interrogate, inter the deep end…
 in the first and last place? Excuse me, I sneezed.
 Merci, you blessed me. Bertie?

Okay Row-yer-boatie, let’s try this (gets out his compass)
 —Oh don’t, please Bertie, not another circle again!—
 Calm yer sen brethren, this is a middling one.

This circle significant eyes a theoretickle solocation
 wherefrom the line itself is a term, say word say,
 and the inside and outside are relata.

The minimal relations, call them tiny dada andna
 minimammy. Can’t thee word sun now shine?
 Cannot the daughter of thou world hersen devine?

I don’t see why not. Can I call you Umbert?
 I read the silence both as aquiescene and an
 invitation         to actually         think.

I’ll say yes, but sigh maybe. Think myself this
 word-child, child word. Wonder, which is tiny dada?
 Who is minimammy? Outside or indershins?

Prick myself with a point of origin again, somewhen
 deep in the still present past within my apparesence.
 You can be tiny dada, but I doubt you’ll be my first word.

Me-ne-ma-ma, did I utter you? Were you not just outside
 but what there was before there was outside?
 The outside outside, the conditions for all this to begin

to begin with? The plain old plane before it was
 scrabbled at? Umbert, this is just the same old
 circle mush, whodya fink you wuz kiddink?

The idea, Row-yer-boatie, was first to see this
 child first, like you did in a way, creating
 its parents. Then what?

Am I supposed to acknowledge that each of them
 must have been somebodings child? Easy to see
 as some kind of tree, say we’re all circles say,

in the end, and looking back unto our roots is
 endless branches. Endless branches.
 This is a kind of edge, isn’t it? Vertiginous

Abyssish. The branches branch, split, keep
 curling curling wider — how could that not
 come back around? Not knot? Me up? Your turn?

Silence. Aye. This could be an edge. And the other?
 Seemed harder to see. If tiny dada was my original
 prick, could he have had a child inside? Have been

a child once too? Wouldn’t that make me
 His Mother? The minimammy of he? Now it is she
 who seems tricksy, never content to lie between,

ever-displaced by pricks and their boundaries,
 chaos of the keeps on being Cosmic Context.
 Context with no content but my discontent.

But how could discontent be Cosmic? I’m a fog.
 This isn’t the tree you’ve given me. I’m a pond
 and it’s a pebble. What if

I am a pond and it is a pebble? How could I then
 be the ripple? Be the ripple? The ripple? Ripple.
 Hmmm. I see what you did there.

Silence. What happened then. I felt it. But it won’t
 satisfy me to think it. I already know it. I know it
 already. I’ll try. It’s happening. I don’t know it at all.

A disappearing pebble. Say they all are in the end.
 ever rippling ripple? No, if anything ever was
 it was still the pond. The still pond. A ripple of feeling

making a little music at the edge, a lapping tongue
 at the beach of no one, big lovely solid cup clay world.
 She must be that thing they mean by Objective Reality.

But there is nothing subjective about the pond.
 We really do ripple, not just or even just not with pebbles
 but with frogs, raindrops, the ticklish feet of upside down

Row-yer-boatie boatmen. We’re all alive and ripply ripply.
 I’m a happy pond. Plenty going on. I must have come from rain
 and to rain I return and by rain I am replenished.

And from under. I am a little showing of a deeper level.
 An ocean under the land. This whole thing is moist.
 Until it seems dry, and I become small and bitter

dark like tea. A stainer of paper. Maybe sometimes
 I completely disappear. Evaporate. A different me
 fills their cup, if it wasn’t mud or reeds or dust,

or wasn’t for someone maintaining me, needing me.
 You who hear the bunny honking in their sleep maybe.
 Maybe you understand me? Umbert? Is that you?

Is this silence? This utterly non-subjective pond
 whose ripples are only a kind of sound to the right
 kind of ear. How do those unappearing pebbles appear?

The ones that cause the ripples only no one can hear?
 More delicate than duck weed roots, less obviously war-
 ping than the light green lentils of the…

surface of me? On the place I end that is not the cupped
 container of my bottom. Sky source and earth source
 and back and forth to so many roots and branches…

such a great tangle of sense. Of senses. Does the pond
 does the pond think? Not just while rippling, of ripples.
 Not just of still, while rippling, of rippling still.

The pond is thinking of still while it is still
 because when it no longer is, neither still
 nor rippling, it cannot think but only cycle,

not be becoming but be potential. And but
 if that is what it always was. No, I am lost.
 The thoughts are all just ripples, but the still

is not just still. It is forever. All ways.
 This is the thought that is the pebble
 that does not cause a ripple. The word

has an origin but no cause, has an end
 without effect. Is this the edge? The edge
 of the interdependent web, or net…

Or neither web nor net. Not if such
 is the jewel
 of ev’ry nexus, and such

is the centre
 and the edge
 of the web

and the silk is just the spider
 of the child of the rainbow of the word
 unweaving itself and weaving itself.

Umbert? No flies on you? No flight
 nor fall? No way to be alone but this nor not to be?
 This is the why. Umbert. Isn’t it?

Isn’t this the why? The why of the way?
 The why of the gently? Gently down the stream?
 There is no reason and yet this is its origin.

The light in which it makes perfect sense.
 The non-light of which there is no nonsense.
 Excepting that which is literally poetry, of course,

Row-yer-boatie.
 Heheheh.
 Umbert.
