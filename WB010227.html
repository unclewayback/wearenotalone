Broken spells are not for casting
 for broken spells cannot be broken:
 A voice for the voiceless would a loss.
 That broken spells cannot be broken
 is neither a fix nor a thing to fixate on.
 That broken spells are not for casting
 makes them as fragile as the truth
 that you are
 everlastin

Oh, I finished the Tàin last night — too much fighting for me in the end. Good to read but we ain’t going back there.

Love is a little like single-combat sometimes, that’s about as close as I can get to endorsing any beauty or glory I did find in it. Which I did, just in the end it didn’t really seem to be about that. Fuck being about, I know, at least there were some gems among us. I don’t want to change it or them, I’m not really judging them, I’m saying how I feel now. Those battlefields are just too much. Who wants to live in a world where virtually every placename commemorates a slaying? Maybe more than once upon a time these will be… something else. But still.

There are animals between us and hell, working just the same way real saints and other beat poets do in the human world. Seeking an end to what? If it feels like everything, we still have work to do. They say good deeds can help. Pretty sure it’s not the size that counts, nor any other way the world tends to measure. You know the score.
