I answered rather crabbily that it is not currently that kind of activity that is exhausting me. It is this. All this talking to you. Brain stuff as some would see it, but to me a kind of metabolism on a soulular level. Passing things back and forth all the time across invisible — sorry, invisabubble — boundaries, so-called.

I thought back to that phrase Doctor Blake led me too: the awe-thrall truth. It is true, I am in thrall. A kind of slave. It is very different from enchantment or being enchanted, which is a holiday compared to this.

I realize that I genuinely am not trying to convince anyone, even myself, of anything, here. That’s not what all this talk of truth and such is about. I still feel the energy of it is wrong somehow, something somewhere is twisted or I have some lines crossed. I do need to relax, let it be, let it circulate. The binds are not chains, necessarily. It is somehow a necessary slavery. It concerns a way it is. So relax, don’t fight it.

Perhaps I can’t ignore it, but I can’t do much about it either, excepting trying to adjust my… figurations? Let me take a step back a minute.

Things that come up like The Meaning of Realization. I know what that is, on some level, but I haven’t expressed it, or perhaps metaphorealized it, to my satisfaction.

You pick up a piece of string, tie a knot in it, glue the ends together, and that is a model of a mathematical knot. The mathematical knot is just a mathematical knot. Not really my subject either. I am interested in its metaphorealization. Perhaps this is in the other direction from the piece of string, but probably that’s too pat.

The thought that I should acknowledge that what I am doing, or dealing with, is intuition. The feeling it is not somehow. That we’ll get to that.

Something else.

The ‘shift’ or ‘change’, i.e. in fact simply an utter discontinuity, between a simple loop, the unknot say, and a knot. To move between them on their own plane, we have to cut. We cut the string, tie or untie the knot, heal the ends back together. DNA is doing this kind of thing all the time, apparently. Poor megacomplex tangle wrapped into a tiny space and it has to keep on reconfiguring itself, so it has to be able to cut and heal its own thread, the thread of itself.

But, also, we can take the knot up to the fourth dimension, perform those operations, and then come back down. It is more on the level of this, that I need to understand, than for instance the polynomials, the mathematical equations that knots can be represented by. I may have to grasp a little of them, in order to understand this, I suppose.

It may be I already have the picture I need, and well enough grasp it, for it is rather straightforward in its way, albeit peculiarly unsatisfying to me. If you spend some time with a Klein bottle you will see it. But a Klein bottle already is a four dimensional structure: a two dimensional surface in a four dimensional space; whereas a knot is a one dimensional line in a three dimensional space. When we look at or project a Klein bottle into three dimensional space it appears to intersect itself. I guess, in 3d, it does. One way of showing how it does not do this in 4d, is to consider it temporally, as if the fourth dimension was time. Then, rather simply, the entirety of the object is not present at the same time, our attention travels a section of it, and thus at the time when it intersects itself in 3d, in 4d itself is not there. Only one part of it is, say the going, but the coming will only be there when it gets to the coming. Wrong analogy, because its all one direction we travel in, but apt, for me personally, somehow.

It is like this. What I am ‘intuiting’.

But it is also like things it is not. It is like the Block Universe. As if the Universe including time was a higher dimensional object or sculpture. Imagine yourself as the cross section of a worm that is everywhere you have been and everywhere you ever will be. Intuitively easy and, to some, appealing. I don’t like this precisely because it is close to what I feel, and yet precisely in that proximity, farther than just saying banana or spilling a bucket of water on the floor.

One avenue is in the starts and ends of our threads. We could mark a biological beginning point when our parent’s cells fused into our first cell, or bunch of them, whatever it is. We could see tributaries of our bio-stream branching off into our children before the great river of the mass of us went out into the desert and evaporated. But, obviously enough, this is a limited metaphor — analogy, really — based on an understanding of ourselves as biological or genetic information. That’s not it.

We can try the same thing with cause and effect on other planes. Not it, not really.

Cultural inputs and outputs.

Nope. Doesn’t really matter what kind of information we’re modelling or thinking in terms of. Not it.

But it is a bit like an object which has a complete state but at any one moment — or place — is only partially present to itself. It is a bit like that. A bit like time. A bit like a book, how it is all there but cannot be read all at once and yet to read it all is an all at once in a way, no matter how long it takes or how discontinuous the process may seem.

But that’s not it. Closer to how it feels, or has felt, say… to me.

I’ve missed something, skipped over something, said wait a minute to some little person, and now I’m not sure where they’ve gone. Hello? Do you want to say something before I try to carry on? Maybe they’ll jump in, seems I need to get into the next bit…

That it is what I mean or intuit or begin to express in the concept or idea of hyperdimensional touch. It is contact. A kind of. Ultraterrestrial Encounter. Close, and very far at the same time.

It is a seeing blind. A special sacred kind of blindness which is actually a way or form of seeing.

Honestly, I feel as if the Blind God, when we do not demonise Him, is part of this. It is here, he really means.

The gnostics, some of them, talked about Him as an Abortion. A living abortion. A kind of unintended consequence of Sophia’s daring (somewhat heedless) plunge. That’s not quite the story I get. It’s not exactly Earth, that I feel is the centre of this, anymore than I am.

I saw, became, the Great Universal Abortion, myself. Just as I have been on the operating table of the Greys, that for me is an operative plateau. And there I did something Christ also did, in a different way. I blazed like Albion Rose, with the heat — and apparent pride, though it is anything but, rather a total openness — of Lucifer. In stillness and silence my hands became arthritic prayer and I was the Universe as an Ancient Unborn Baby. Unbirthable. Start is linked to end.

What does it mean: If the doors of perception were cleansed, we would see everything as it is: infinite?

What is that infinity? What is the infinity of any particular — particuliar — ‘thing’ ?

You see, or feel, that this is a wound of me. For me. I feel very conflicted about sharing it. I know that I cannot, really, share it. And cannot not.

If you want to understand the character of Uncle Wayback, though, you need to see this. At least see it in or through him. So it is necessary, for this work.

I guess I shouldn’t be too sorry. It is not so different to the dying god. To being grateful for or to something in and beyond that icon of suffering who is the crucified christ. It is not really a pleasant gift. It is and remains a wound, a perpetual wound. There is no beauty without this sublime human-divine hurting. Every…

I don’t know, I’m thinking of those very feeling-full classical musical movements, those wordless swells of bringing tears to our eyes, how the immortal wounding underwrites them. But they are somehow too clean or obvious, or simply classical, an example.

That is a rich and good conundrum, for me, a fertile place that animates much of this quest of mine. The creation, or discovery in myself, somewhat as myself… of a less classical example.

May every seat be throne.

It is not me I come to like this, understand that. Who I come to is the female presence I call Amulet. In a somewhat literal way, the knots are like amulets, to me. They are something we share, me and her, something we are looking at together, perhaps contacting each other through, or else a way of us understanding what or how that is.

It might just be me, a way of dealing with some of the excess energy of it, or doing something in the meantime. Maybe it is just a metaphorealization of the process of figuring out… looking in and figuring out.

I honestly don’t know, and I am sorry if I give a false impression of knowing. I have talked about what knowing is for me, though, and that is feeling, essentially. It doesn’t help, for some reason, to call this intuition. Perhaps it is not really what we think of as that, even though it might be an aspect of what that really is.

Anyway, one way of thinking about Amulet, and understanding Wayback, is to see her as the daughter he never had. You can perhaps empathise with that, but you would be wrong if it made you feel at all sorry for him.

It may be that she agreed to stay behind — beyond — in order to do this work with him. Tether, apart from being related both to the concept of physical and metaphysical and likely also magical Aether, and apart from its relation to the Akashic Field, thus Records… it is exemplified most purely in relations such as this: the connection between Uncle Wayback and Amulet.

Maybe she is simply a crazy person like him, in the future or in a parallel world, and this is how they bond. She may also not be human. There is another way I see her sometimes that would seem to suggest not. This is on one level a quite simple geometry of two circles within a more squashed or ovaling third (or first, but to be honest if such orderings exist they are meaningful and not temporal relationships). On another level it is two hands, that move from something like fists to something like a fan of open hands. On another level it is a kind of cute Grey alien, more like E.T. though. I have seen her as a wreath in a tree… I might just be confused.

You understand, however, that there is a specificity to or within this confusion. Likely this is part of the constellation particuliarity.

One important point I must make is that it would be wrong — I think so, of course you are free to think otherwise or otherwise disagree — to reduce this to a kind of trauma. A traumatic absence in my life. My having failed some deep part of myself by not having produced a child or children. I do explore and have explored these aspects to my own satisfaction. Certainly they are part of it. But these are parts of how it works, for me. It is these powerful psycho-physical binds that are part of what fix me to what, with due attention and energy, then becomes a revelation or realization of a higher order. Trauma is a gateway or portal. It doesn’t reduce to that excepting the way in which something like a painting or a picture can be said to act as or be a portal. Similar to the way we might understand poetry as meaningfully inexhaustible — only it would have to be something integral to the poetry and not merely given to it by the shifting circumstances of context including both individual perspectives and the nature of time. In other words the meaning is inexhaustible NOT because every individual has their own approach and set of interpretations AND NOT because the world is always moving or changing in time. The picture is a picture of infinity. It is a knot. And not the trivial one we employ as a symbol for it! (Though it could be for God.)

I feel very alone in this. Maybe that is part of the trauma that produces the agitation necessary for these parts or aspects of my diary or log. But I don’t know. Amulet is part of this aloneness, not really a relief of or from it. Nothing is. Apart from it, somehow.

It is quite possible, I do hereby seriously entertain the possibility, that not many people are or ever will be capable of seeing this in a way that they actually realize they are. Seeing blind, so to speak, or else engaging ones potential for hyperdimensional touch. That is Portal Tech, I guess. I tend to accidentally write poetential in such cases!

This does make me a kind of Genius. It is also traumatic and difficult for me to admit or confront this. Particularly because I am a dumb Genius. Hence the term, perhaps slightly offensive to some, Wiztard. I can’t do anything with or about this, short of what I’m doing. I am the fool, and not always the funny one. One of the names for the Demiurge, the great demon, is Saklas: Blind One, Fool. This is not not me. I am not not The Black Fool. I am insanity, and my only medicine is madness. That’s why I’m sorry.
