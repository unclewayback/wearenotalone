January 3rd, 2023

07:33

Couldn’t read me Dante last night. Couldn’t read nuffink, in fact. Wondered if I really wanted to? Maybe one of them stories I was on about… but really, thought I should be sat there with a doodle, driftin off with a doodle. Stayin sharp as a pencil.

I’m living inside this computer at the moment. Want to stop, draw back. But I’ve got to look at the screen in order to one day not have to look at the screen so much, it says. Take apart your old forays now, and salvage what you can of them. Make them nice and modular. Modularize them. Start leaving yourself the notes you wish you had then. Who would have thought it was this easy to forget how something you made was made?

Stop this waffle. Poor Diarist. Wrist? Maybeep. If all of these words are the not being of other ones you’d rather write. Just keep us up-to-date if there is something to be kept up about, matey. That includes if it is just if yer hahahappy, of course. Otherwise, head down. Serious clown.

Grim fool? No, y dick’edd. Wonkleton Wyrdo. Waffledom Clamb. Not very grim, really. Though, it is funny, the blackness of peace. The undetermined anythink necessary of its not doing ness. Yae woodnae mess.

Cudnae!
